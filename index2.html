Estimating Software iPad and Web App

Schneider Electric one of the worlds biggest electrical manufacturers needed to replace their outdated estimating software. The system needed to allow a service engineer to create and manage quotes and have them synced on both a native iPad app and a webapp.

In addition the quote needed to access and update service order information stored within Schneider Electric ERP and authenticate against PING ID their two factor authentication server.

My Role

Lead designer, business analyst and project manager.
Scrum master, responsible for a team of three full stack developers.

Deliverables

I was responsible for collection and understanding of business requirements and logic, competitive research, user interviews, personas, prototypes and presentation.
